# Problem Understanding

## Business Problem

When a bank gives a loan, there is always a risk that the client may not pay it back. This can create losses for the bank.

## Project Objective

In this project, the goal is to build a machine learning model that predicts if a client is a good or bad credit risk.

## Problem Type

This is a supervised classification problem because the model learns from past data and predicts one of two classes:
- good credit risk
- bad credit risk

## Why It Matters

This is important because banks need to make good decisions before giving a loan. A model like this can help reduce risk and avoid losses.
